# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

`starSling` aims to keep discussions direct, technical, and respectful.

Participants in this project are expected to:

- communicate in good faith
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior rather than people
- respect different levels of experience and different technical backgrounds
- keep bug reports, reviews, and design discussions factual and actionable

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions:

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or insulting language
- repeated bad-faith argument, trolling, or deliberate disruption
- publishing private information without permission
- sharing credentials, secrets, or sensitive operational details in public threads

## Maintainer Responsibility

Project maintainers may moderate, edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and contributions that conflict with this code of conduct.

Maintainers may also restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately at `muzexlxl@foxmail.com`.

When possible, include:

- a link or screenshot
- the relevant issue, pull request, or discussion
- a brief description of what happened

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- the GitHub repository
- issues and pull requests
- review comments and discussions
- other project-managed communication channels related to `starSling`
